About: My Game

My main project is a Java console game called LunarVerse that I have coded using Eclipse. It features two players going head to head in a turn based strategy game inspired from Mario & Rabbids: Sparks of Hope. Both players control 3 characters and use their weapons, abilities, and ultimates in order to take down the other team. The characters in the game are the same ones from my books.

LunarVerse Characters

A breakdown on the general kits of each character. Check out all the characters here!

  • Roles: Characters have 1-3 roles that give them passive buffs, and define their general playstyle.
  • Movement: Characters can move a certain number of tiles each turn. Jumps and dashes can be used during movement.
  • Weapon: A character's weapon is their typical way of dishing out damage to the enemy characters. Can be used once a turn.
  • Ability: A character's ability can provide general support to the team. Abilities go on a set cooldown after use.
  • Ultimate: A character's ultimate is a powerful game changing tool that can be earned by collecting orbs to fufill the ultimate charge.

General Tutorial

Use your weapons to hurt the enemies. Movement is crucial for reposition to cover and getting orbs. Jumps are vital for escaping dangerous situations. Dashes are great for taking down utility and dealing extra damage. Get in abilities early to have more value and have them ready for the late game. Ultimates can turn the tide of the game, time them right and don‚Äôt hold on to them for too long. Study the enemy team well to see their weaknesses and strengths.
